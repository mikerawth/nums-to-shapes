Instructions

  1. Sort an array of integers in ascending order
  2. Integer divisible by 2 is a square
  3. Integer divisible by 3 is a circle
  4. Integer divisable by 2 & 3 is a triangle
  5. Integer is even, shape should be red
  6. Integer is odd, shape should be green

Technical Requirements

  • Sort integers using the array.sort() method
  • Stating that an integer is divisible by 2 & 3 means that it is divisble by 6
  • To produce the correct result, check to see that an integer is divisble by 6 before checking an integer is divisble by 2
    Checking to see that an integer is divisble by 2 first means that the function will never check for an integer divisble by 6
  • All integers divisible by 2 & 6 will be even and thus red
  • All integers divisble by 3, but not 6, will be odd and thus green
  • Also need to include a shape that is not divisible by 2,3 or 6. This includes numbers divisible by 5, 7 and other prime numbers (11, 13, etc.).
    Since a number divisible by 2 will always be even, numbers that are not included in the above criteria will always be odd.
